About Digital Payment Index


UPI touched record high of 6 billion transactions worth 10 trillion recently

But daily tracking of these numbers is cumbersome and these numbers are getting too big to put our heads around it.

Many feel tracking these numbers like stock market index such as NIFTY, SENSEX etc.. will make the everyone's life easier

This product does exactly that by tracking DPI Index which not only includes UPI but also NEFT, RTGS, IMPS, FASTag and NACH
